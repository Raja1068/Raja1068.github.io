Convert PDF Tables to Excel in Seconds

Our free online PDF to Excel converter makes it easy to extract tables from PDF documents and convert them to fully editable Excel spreadsheets. Perfect for financial reports, data analysis, and any PDF containing tabular data.

Why Convert PDF to Excel?

  • Edit data easily: PDFs are difficult to edit - Excel makes data manipulation simple
  • Save time: No more manual data entry from PDF documents
  • Analyze data: Use Excel's powerful functions and formulas on your extracted data
  • Preserve formatting: Our converter maintains table structure and formatting

How to Convert PDF to Excel

  1. Upload your PDF file by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Select your preferred output format (XLSX, CSV, or ODS)
  3. Click "Convert to Excel" to process your file
  4. Preview the extracted tables and download your Excel file

Supported PDF Types
