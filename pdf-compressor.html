Optimize Your PDFs for Better Sharing

Our free online PDF compression tool helps you reduce the file size of your PDF documents while maintaining quality. Smaller PDFs are easier to email, upload, and share with others.

Why Compress PDFs?

  • Email attachments: Many email providers limit attachment sizes
  • Faster uploads: Smaller files upload quicker to websites and cloud services
  • Save storage space: Reduce space used on your device or server
  • Better sharing: Compressed PDFs are easier to send via messaging apps

How to Use Our PDF Compressor

  1. Upload your PDF by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Select your compression preferences
  3. Click "Compress PDF" to process your file
  4. Download the optimized PDF

How PDF Compression Works

Our tool uses smart algorithms to reduce PDF file size by optimizing images, removing duplicate resources, and compressing text and vector elements. The higher the compression level you choose, the smaller your file will be, though very high compression may slightly reduce quality.
